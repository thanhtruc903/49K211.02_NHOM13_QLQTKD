Sales Process Analysis & Improvement – Gau Kafe
Project Overview
This project analyzes and improves the sales and customer service process at Gau Kafe, a coffee shop. The goal is to identify inefficiencies in the current workflow and propose solutions to enhance operational efficiency and customer experience.
Business Problem
The existing sales process at the coffee shop had several issues such as unclear workflows, potential delays in order processing, and limited coordination between staff during peak hours. These issues could affect service speed and customer satisfaction.
Methodology
The project applied Business Process Management (BPM) techniques to analyze and improve the workflow:
•	Conducted interviews with store staff to understand the current process.
•	Modeled the AS-IS business process using BPMN.
•	Identified root causes of inefficiencies using:
o	Pareto Chart
o	Fishbone Diagram
o	5 Whys analysis
•	Proposed improvements by applying concepts from POS, KDS, and CRM systems.
Process Simulation
The improved process was simulated using BIMP to evaluate performance metrics such as processing time, efficiency, and operational cost.
Tools & Technologies
•	Draw.io – BPMN modeling
•	BPMN – Business process modeling
•	BIMP – Process simulation
Project Outcome
•	Identified key bottlenecks in the sales workflow.
•	Proposed an improved process model to optimize order handling and service coordination.
•	Demonstrated potential improvements in operational efficiency through process simulation.
